Leo's Famous Meatloaf

Not a lot of peole clamor over meatloaf. Ugh. But this particular recipe has been honed into perfection to provide a flavorful taste profile that keeps on giving. Give it a try!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (preferably an 70/30 mix for extra fatty content)
  • 1 lb ground venison
  • 12 oz bread crumbs (I use plain, but feel free to experiment with other flavors!)
  • 1 cup diced bell peppers
  • .3 cup diced jalapeno
  • 1 medium white onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • .5 cup milk
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 16 oz ketchup
  • 1 tbsp cayenne pepper
  • 8 oz light brown sugar
  • .2 cup red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Begin with combining milk and bread crumbs into a bowl. Set aside for later
  2. Heat a large skillet to medium high heat, combine diced peppers, onion, garlic and salt and pepper. Let this cook for roughly 5 minutes to release aromatic flavoris.
  3. Once finished, set aside into large bowl.
  4. In the same large bowl, combine beef and venison with the eggs.
  5. Mix this well.
  6. Combine with mixture with the bread crumbs which should be soaked by now.
  7. Mix this well.
  8. Shape this mixture into a log on a baking sheet.
  9. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
  10. Insert meat log into oven, letting cook for one half hours or until 160 degrees F internal.
  11. While meat cooks, combine ketchup, brown sugar, cayenne and vinegar for later.
  12. When meat is done, cover with ketchup glaze, paying close attention to full coverage.
  13. Cook this for another 30 minutes or until glaze is carmelized and browned.
  14. Slice and serve hot.
